Department of Leadership and Management

Join our Department of Leadership and Management under the School of Business and Management and develop the skills, knowledge, and expertise necessary to become a successful leader in today's fast-paced business environment. Our programs focus on leadership development, strategic management, and organizational behavior.

What You'll Gain
  • In-depth knowledge of leadership and management principles, practices, and theories
  • Practical skills in leadership, communication, problem-solving, and decision-making
  • Ability to analyze organizational problems and develop effective solutions
  • Understanding of global leadership trends, practices, and technologies
  • Insights into entrepreneurship, innovation, and sustainability
Levels of study
  • Certificate in Leadership and Management
  • Diploma in Leadership and Management
  • Bachelor of Science in Leadership and Management
  • Master of Science in Leadership and Management
  • Executive Certificate in Leadership and Management
