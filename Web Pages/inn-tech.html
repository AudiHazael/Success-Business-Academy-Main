Department of Innovation and Technology

Join our Department of Innovation and Technology under the School of Technology and Innovation and develop the skills, knowledge, and expertise necessary to succeed in innovation and technology management. Our programs focus on innovation strategy, technology management, entrepreneurship, and digital transformation.

What You'll Gain
  • In-depth knowledge of innovation and technology principles, practices, and theories
  • Practical skills in innovation strategy, technology management, and entrepreneurship
  • Ability to analyze complex technological problems and develop effective solutions
  • Understanding of global technological trends, practices, and technologies
  • Insights into digital transformation, innovation leadership, and technology entrepreneurship
Levels of study
  • Certificate in Innovation and Technology
  • Diploma in Innovation and Technology
  • Bachelor of Science in Innovation and Technology
  • Master of Science in Innovation and Technology
  • Master of Science in Technology Management
  • Executive Certificate in Innovation and Technology
